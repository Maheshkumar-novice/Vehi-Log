Registration & Renewal Process
New Vehicle Registration
  1. Within 7 days of purchase
  2. Visit nearest RTO office
  3. Submit required documents
  4. Pay registration fee
  5. Vehicle inspection
  6. Get number plate
  7. Receive RC book
Registration Fees
  • Two-Wheeler: ₹300-500
  • Four-Wheeler: ₹600-1,000
  • Smart Card: ₹50
  • Number Plate: ₹350
RC Renewal Process
  1. After 15 years for first renewal
  2. Every 5 years thereafter
  3. Apply 6 months before expiry
  4. Submit Form 25 + documents
  5. Pay renewal fee
  6. Vehicle fitness test
  7. Get renewed RC
Renewal Fees
  • Two-Wheeler: ₹200-400
  • Four-Wheeler: ₹400-800
  • Fitness Test: ₹200
  • Late Fee: ₹25/month
Inter-State Transfer (NOC)
NOC Process
  1. Apply at current RTO
  2. Submit Form 28 + documents
  3. Clear all pending challans
  4. Pay NOC fee
  5. Get NOC certificate
  6. Register at new state RTO
  7. Get new registration number
NOC Requirements
  • Original RC book
  • Insurance certificate
  • PUC certificate
  • Address proof of new state
  • Tax clearance certificate
  • ID proof
  • Passport size photos
Important: NOC is valid for 6 months. Registration must be completed within this period.

Important Dates
Registration Validity
  • First Registration: 15 years
  • Subsequent Renewals: 5 years
  • Commercial Vehicles: 10 years
Tax Payment
  • Annual: Before 31st March
  • One-time: At registration
  • Quarterly: For commercial
Grace Period
  • Tax Payment: 30 days
  • RC Renewal: 30 days
  • Late Fee: ₹25/month
Penalties
Common Violations
  • No Registration: ₹5,000
  • Expired Registration: ₹1,000
  • Tax Evasion: ₹10,000
  • Fake Documents: ₹25,000
  • Unauthorized Modifications: ₹5,000
Repeat Offences
  • Fine: Double the amount
  • Suspension: License/Registration
  • Imprisonment: Up to 6 months
Tips
  • Keep documents updated
  • Pay tax on time
  • Use online services
  • Keep payment receipts
  • Check validity dates
  • Clear challans promptly
