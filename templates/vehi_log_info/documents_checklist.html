Documents Checklist

Vehicle Registration Documents
For New Vehicle Registration
  • Form 20 - Application for registration
  • Form 21 - Sale certificate from dealer
  • Invoice - Original purchase invoice
  • Insurance Policy - Valid insurance certificate
  • PAN Card - PAN card copy
  • Address Proof - Aadhaar/Passport/Electricity bill
  • Pollution Certificate - PUC certificate
Driving License Documents
For Learner's License
  • Form 2 - Application for learner's license
  • Age Proof - Birth certificate/10th marksheet
  • Address Proof - Aadhaar/Passport/Utility bill
  • Photographs - Passport size photos
  • Medical Certificate - For commercial license
For Permanent License
  • Form 4 - Application for permanent license
  • Learner's License - Valid learner's license
  • Training Certificate - From recognized driving school
Insurance Documents
For Insurance Purchase
  • RC Copy - Vehicle registration certificate
  • Driving License - Valid driving license
  • Previous Policy - For renewal cases
  • NCB Certificate - No claim bonus certificate
  • Vehicle Photos - Current vehicle photos
For Claim Settlement
  • Policy Copy - Insurance policy certificate
  • FIR Copy - Police complaint (if required)
  • Repair Bills - Garage repair estimates
  • Bank Details - For claim settlement
Transfer Documents
For Vehicle Transfer
  • Form 29 - NOC from previous owner
  • Form 30 - Application for transfer
  • Original RC - Vehicle registration certificate
  • Sale Agreement - Sale deed between parties
  • Insurance Transfer - Insurance policy transfer
  • Address Proof - New owner's address proof
Documents to Carry While Driving
Mandatory
  • Valid Driving License
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate
  • Insurance Certificate
  • Pollution Certificate
Recommended
  • Permit (if commercial)
  • Fitness Certificate
  • Tax Receipt
  • Emergency Contact Numbers
Digital Copies
  • mParivahan App
  • DigiLocker App
  • Scanned copies in phone
  • Cloud storage backup
Emergency Kit
  • First Aid Kit
  • Fire Extinguisher
  • Warning Triangle
  • Basic Tools
Important Tips
Document Management
  • Keep originals safe at home
  • Carry certified copies while driving
  • Maintain digital backups
  • Regular document renewal
  • Update address changes promptly
Renewal Schedule
  • Driving License: 20 years (new), 10 years (renewal)
  • Insurance: 1 year (mandatory renewal)
  • PUC: 6 months (petrol), 3 months (diesel)
  • RC: 15 years (first), 5 years (subsequent)
  • Fitness: 15 years (private), 10 years (commercial)
