Seasonal Maintenance Tips

Summer Care (April-June)
Temperature: 35-45°C
Engine Care
  • Check coolant level weekly
  • Use summer-grade engine oil
  • Clean radiator fins
  • Check fan belt tension
  • Monitor engine temperature
Tyre Maintenance
  • Check pressure weekly
  • Reduce pressure by 2-3 PSI
  • Avoid overloading
  • Park in shade
Air Conditioning
  • Service AC system
  • Clean/replace cabin filter
  • Check refrigerant level
  • Use AC regularly
Battery Care
  • Check water level
  • Clean terminals
  • Keep battery cool
  • Check charging system
Monsoon Care (July-September)
Rainfall: Heavy to Very Heavy
Electrical System
  • Check all lights
  • Test wiper blades
  • Inspect electrical connections
  • Clean battery terminals
  • Check horn functionality
Braking System
  • Check brake pads
  • Test brake fluid
  • Clean brake drums
  • Check brake cables
Waterproofing
  • Seal door/window gaps
  • Apply rust protection
  • Check exhaust system
  • Protect electrical parts
Filters & Fluids
  • Clean air filter frequently
  • Change fuel filter
  • Check transmission fluid
  • Use quality fuel
Winter Care (October-March)
Temperature: 15-25°C
Engine Starting
  • Allow proper warm-up
  • Use winter-grade oil
  • Check spark plugs
  • Keep fuel tank full
  • Check choke operation
Battery Performance
  • Test battery capacity
  • Check charging system
  • Clean terminals
  • Keep battery warm
Tyre Care
  • Check tread depth
  • Maintain proper pressure
  • Check for cracks
  • Rotate tyres
Visibility
  • Clean headlights
  • Check all bulbs
  • Clean mirrors
  • Test defogger

Weather-Specific Precautions
Summer Precautions
  • Never ignore overheating
  • Avoid driving in peak heat
  • Keep extra coolant/water
  • Check AC before summer
  • Park in shade when possible
Monsoon Precautions
  • Avoid waterlogged areas
  • Drive slowly on wet roads
  • Keep emergency kit ready
  • Check weather before travel
  • Dry vehicle after rain
Winter Precautions
  • Allow engine warm-up
  • Check battery regularly
  • Keep fuel tank full
  • Clean fog lights
  • Check heating system
