Expense Categories Guide

Default Categories
Fuel

Includes: Petrol, Diesel, CNG refueling

Frequency: Weekly/Bi-weekly

Average Cost: ₹500-2,000 per fill

Service

Includes: Regular maintenance, oil changes, tune-ups

Frequency: Every 3-6 months

Average Cost: ₹1,500-5,000

Repairs

Includes: Breakdown repairs, emergency fixes

Frequency: As needed

Average Cost: ₹500-10,000+

Insurance

Includes: Premium payments, policy renewals

Frequency: Annual

Average Cost: ₹2,000-15,000

Registration

Includes: RC renewal, license fees, RTO charges

Frequency: Annual/Bi-annual

Average Cost: ₹500-3,000

Accessories

Includes: Seat covers, mirrors, lights, modifications

Frequency: Occasional

Average Cost: ₹200-5,000

Toll

Includes: Highway tolls, parking fees

Frequency: Travel dependent

Average Cost: ₹50-500 per trip

Tyres

Includes: Tyre replacement, puncture repair, alignment

Frequency: 2-3 years

Average Cost: ₹1,000-8,000

Parts

Includes: Brake pads, filters, spark plugs, batteries

Frequency: As needed

Average Cost: ₹200-5,000

Cleaning

Includes: Car wash, detailing, interior cleaning

Frequency: Weekly/Monthly

Average Cost: ₹100-1,000
