Fuel Efficiency Tips

Driving Habits
Good Habits
  • Maintain steady speed
  • Gentle acceleration
  • Anticipate traffic
  • Use cruise control on highways
  • Turn off engine at long signals
  • Plan routes to avoid traffic
Bad Habits
  • Rapid acceleration
  • Hard braking
  • Excessive speeding
  • Frequent lane changes
  • Riding clutch
  • Long idling
Vehicle Maintenance
Regular Maintenance
  • Engine Oil: Change every 5,000 km
  • Air Filter: Clean/replace every 10,000 km
  • Spark Plugs: Replace every 15,000 km
  • Fuel Filter: Clean every 20,000 km
  • Tyre Pressure: Check monthly
  • Wheel Alignment: Check every 6 months
Fuel System Care
  • Fuel Quality: Use branded fuel stations
  • Fuel Additives: Use occasionally
  • Carburetor: Clean every 15,000 km
  • Fuel Injectors: Clean every 30,000 km
  • Fuel Tank: Don't run on empty
  • Fuel Line: Check for leaks
Seasonal Tips
Summer (April-June)
  • Park in shade when possible
  • Use AC efficiently
  • Check coolant levels
  • Maintain proper tyre pressure
  • Service air conditioning
Monsoon (July-September)
  • Use quality fuel
  • Check air filter frequently
  • Avoid water-logged areas
  • Maintain steady speed
  • Use lights during rain
Winter (October-March)
  • Allow engine warm-up
  • Check battery condition
  • Use lower viscosity oil
  • Keep fuel tank full
  • Check heating system

Quick Tips
  • Optimal Speed: 40-60 km/h for best mileage
  • Gear Shifting: Shift at 2,000-2,500 RPM
  • Tyre Pressure: Check monthly, maintain as per manual
  • Weight: Remove unnecessary items
  • Route Planning: Use GPS for shortest routes
