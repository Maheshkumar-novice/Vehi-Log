Vehicle Insurance Guide

Types of Insurance
Third Party Insurance

Coverage: Damage to third party property/person

Cost: ₹500-2,000/year

Legal: Mandatory by law

Covers: Third party only

Minimum Required
Comprehensive Insurance

Coverage: Own vehicle + third party

Cost: ₹3,000-15,000/year

Legal: Optional but recommended

Covers: Accident, theft, fire, natural disasters

Recommended
Add-on Covers
Popular Add-ons
  • Zero Depreciation: No depreciation deduction
  • Engine Protection: Covers engine damage
  • Roadside Assistance: 24/7 emergency support
  • Consumables Cover: Covers oils, filters, etc.
  • Key Replacement: Lost key coverage
Specialized Add-ons
  • Personal Accident: Owner-driver coverage
  • Passenger Cover: Covers passengers
  • NCB Protection: Protects no-claim bonus
  • Invoice Protection: Covers ex-showroom price
  • Tyres Protection: Covers tyre damage
Claim Process
Steps to File Claim
  1. Immediate: Inform police (if required)
  2. Call: Insurance company helpline
  3. Document: Take photos, get FIR copy
  4. Submit: Claim form with documents
  5. Survey: Wait for insurance surveyor
  6. Repair: Get vehicle repaired
  7. Settlement: Receive claim amount
Required Documents
  • Insurance policy copy
  • Driving license copy
  • Vehicle registration copy
  • FIR copy (if applicable)
  • Repair bills and estimates
  • Bank account details
  • Claim form (duly filled)
