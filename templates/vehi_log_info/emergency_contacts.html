What to Do in an Emergency
Accident Response
  1. Ensure safety - move to safe location
  2. Call emergency services (100/112)
  3. Provide first aid if trained
  4. Document the scene (photos)
  5. Call insurance company
  6. Get police report if required
  7. Exchange information with other parties
Vehicle Breakdown
  1. Move to safe location
  2. Turn on hazard lights
  3. Set up warning triangle
  4. Call roadside assistance
  5. Stay visible and safe
  6. Keep emergency kit handy
  7. Have important documents ready
