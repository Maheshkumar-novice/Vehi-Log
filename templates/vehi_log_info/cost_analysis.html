Decision Factors
When to Sell/Replace
  • Age: 10+ years for two-wheelers, 12+ years for cars
  • Mileage: 80,000+ km for two-wheelers, 1,50,000+ km for cars
  • Repair Costs: Exceeding 40% of vehicle value annually
  • Fuel Efficiency: Drops below 70% of original
  • Safety: Major safety issues or recalls
  • Technology: Outdated features affecting usability
Cost vs Benefit Analysis
Factor Keep Replace
Annual Repair Cost < 20% of value > 40% of value
Fuel Efficiency > 70% original < 50% original
Downtime < 5 days/year > 15 days/year
Market Value > 30% purchase < 15% purchase
